== Description ==

The Charity Nonprofit theme is a powerful, multipurpose template designed specifically for nonprofit organizations looking to amplify their mission and engage communities. Crafted with modern design and robust functionality, this theme is ideal for nonprofit management, fundraising, and event organization. Whether you are building a nonprofit website, hosting a nonprofit blog, or launching nonprofit marketing campaigns, this theme provides an elegant platform to showcase success stories, promote initiatives, and drive donations. It supports volunteer management, board oversight, and leadership activities, ensuring streamlined operations and enhanced community outreach. Visually, the theme boasts a responsive, retina-ready design with customizable banners, interactive galleries, and vibrant layouts that elevate branding. It is also optimized for SEO and integrates seamlessly with CRM tools, donation forms, and social media channels, enhancing fundraising and digital engagement. With built-in features for analytics, impact measurement, and digital marketing, organizations can effectively track campaigns and refine strategies. Additionally, the Charity Nonprofit theme offers comprehensive support for events management, newsletters, and advocacy, making it an essential asset for any nonprofit dedicated to transparency, innovation, and sustainable community development. This theme is suitable for causes like charity foundations, crowdfunding campaigns, social impact projects, humanitarian aid, disaster relief, animal welfare, food drives, orphanage support, educational support, and environmental campaigns. It is compatible with Give Donation plugin, helping organizations collect online donations with ease, and supports features like recurring donations, donor management, and customizable donation forms. Whether you're running a charitable trust, NGO, religious charity, medical aid program, or a fundraising event, this theme offers everything needed to build trust and drive positive action through a clean, feature-rich, and community-driven digital presence.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
